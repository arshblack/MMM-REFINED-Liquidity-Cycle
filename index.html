The Methodology · Three Pillars
I
Structure
Read the Market's Blueprint

Identify Higher Highs / Higher Lows for bullish and Lower Highs / Lower Lows for bearish. Structure tells you where price wants to go.

  • Break of Structure (BOS)
  • Change of Character (CHoCH)
  • Order Blocks & Fair Value Gaps
II
Liquidity
Follow the Smart Money

Banks need liquidity to fill orders. They engineer moves to sweep stop losses before the real move. Spot the trap before you become it.

  • Equal Highs / Lows = Targets
  • Stop Hunt Mechanics
  • Liquidity Sweep → Reversal
III
Timing
Strike at the Right Moment

The best setups happen during London and NY sessions. Session timing separates professional discretion from gambling.

  • London Open: 07:00 GMT
  • New York Open: 12:00 GMT
  • Killzone Overlap: 12:00–15:00
