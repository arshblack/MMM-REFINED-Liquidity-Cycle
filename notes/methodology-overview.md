# Methodology Overview

This project studies structured market behavior using high-level concepts such as market context, liquidity behavior, session timing, and multi-timeframe alignment.

The public methodology is intentionally general. It does not publish exact signal rules, thresholds, formulas, or execution conditions.

## High-Level Research Themes

- Multi-timeframe market context
- Session-based market observation
- Liquidity and structure analysis
- Trade quality scoring at a conceptual level
- Risk discipline and controlled exposure
- Dashboard-driven review and Telegram alert workflows
- Manual and automated testing review

## What Is Not Shared

The exact model logic, scoring weights, execution rules, and risk formulas remain private.
