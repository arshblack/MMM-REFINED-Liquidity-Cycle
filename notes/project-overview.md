# Project Overview

Gold Smart Money is a public-facing overview of a private MT5 Expert Advisor research project.

The research focuses on building a structured trading assistant that can observe market context, organize decision support, and support disciplined testing. The public documentation is written for collaborators, reviewers, and anyone who needs to understand the project direction without seeing proprietary implementation details.

## Research Scope

- XAUUSD research
- GBPUSD research
- AUDJPY research
- MT5 dashboard and alert workflows
- Strategy Tester and demo-forward validation
- Documentation for repeatable review

## Public Goal

The public goal is to communicate the project clearly while keeping the private trading model protected.
