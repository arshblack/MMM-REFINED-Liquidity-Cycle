# Architecture

The private research system is organized into several broad layers. This note describes the structure at a safe public level only.

## Layers

- Market Context Layer: summarizes broad directional and structural context.
- Observation Layer: tracks non-sensitive market state categories used by the research process.
- Signal Research Layer: evaluates whether conditions are interesting enough for review.
- Risk Control Layer: keeps exposure constrained and avoids unsafe behavior.
- Trade Management Layer: manages positions according to private rules.
- Dashboard and Alert Layer: presents readable chart information and notification summaries.
- Documentation and Testing Layer: supports backtesting notes, demo review, and collaboration.

## Boundary

This repository does not include private source code, exact conditions, or proprietary formulas.
